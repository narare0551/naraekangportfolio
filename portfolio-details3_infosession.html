Organized Global Non Profit Info Session Series

Motivation

I attended the annual summit hosted by the Minnesota International NonProfit Network(MINN). I got to connect with many people workin in International NonProfit Organizations. I got inspired to hold a workshop for students that connects mentors who are already engaged in the NGO industry and students.

Organizing Preparation Process

I initiated and organized a networking event offering international students, and local nonprofit professionals to connect, share, and learn. Through striving to host the high-level and worthwhile event, I demonstrate leadership skills and communication skills with the local community and leaders.

Outcome

Received Culture Corp scholarship as an acknowledgement of organizing Info Session Series
